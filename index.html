Retail Pricing

How a Retailer Recovered $2.1M in Margin Without Raising Prices

Context

A national retail chain with 400 locations was experiencing persistent margin erosion despite stable sales volumes. Pricing decisions were based on quarterly category reviews — far too slow for a dynamic market.

Approach

Built demand elasticity models per SKU cluster using 3 years of POS data, layered with competitor price signals scraped daily. Ran A/B pricing experiments across 8 pilot stores over 90 days.

Outcome

Rolled out dynamic pricing recommendations across all 14,000 SKUs. Gross margin improved 23% YoY with zero increase to shelf price for >80% of items.

SaaS Retention

Predicting Customer Churn 60 Days Before It Happens

Context

A fast-growing B2B SaaS company with 3,000 enterprise accounts was experiencing unexpected churn spikes at renewal. Customer success had no systematic early-warning system.

Approach

Built a survival analysis model using product usage telemetry, support ticket sentiment, and CRM engagement signals. Developed an account health score surfaced in Salesforce dashboards for CS reps.

Outcome

Model achieved 87% precision at 60-day lead time. Proactive outreach campaigns reduced churn by 31% — saving approximately $900K in ARR in the first year of deployment.
